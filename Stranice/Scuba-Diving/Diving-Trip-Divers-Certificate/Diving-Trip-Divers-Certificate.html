Description
Experience the beauty of the Adriatic Sea with our half-day boat diving trips along the stunning Makarska Riviera. If you're a certified diver looking to exolore something new and interesting, our guided boat trips are the perfect way to enjoy the diverse range of dive areas and the magnificent underwater world.

Under the expert guidance of our certified diving guides, you will dive into the deep blue sea and discover the thriving and colorful reefs, coral walls, caves, and wrecks of the area. Our favorite sea creatures that we want you to meet are the Scorpion fish, lobsters, seahorses and many more!
Main Info
Our half-day boat diving trip includes diving on two different dive spots, each with a brand variety of landscapes to explore and plenty of underwater marvels to discover. Some of the famous dive sites include the Kamena plo√∂a coral walls, the Silver Cave of the Punta Rata, and the Komelj wreck.

At the start of the trip, we will meet at our dive centre at 8:00 am, where you will be equipped with adequate diving gear. From there we'll make our way to our diving boat and enjoy the breathtaking landscape and the view of the Biokovo Mountain while heading to our first dive spot.

Before each dive, our experienced instructors will provide a short briefing about the dive spot, ensuring that everyone is prepared and ready to dive safely. After the first dive, we'll have a short break before heading to our second dive spot.

Our team will choose the most suitable dive spots for your level and your wishes, as well as taking into consideration the weather and sea conditions.

We'll return to our starting point around 1 PM with plenty of memories to last a lifetime. Book your half-day boat diving trip with us today and experience the stunning underwater world of the Adriatic sea.
